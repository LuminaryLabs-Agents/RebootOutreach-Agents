# Reboot Imagine Profile

Date: 2026-06-24

## Positioning

Reboot Imagine is a spatial-computing focused managed service provider for XR device operations.

Primary public positioning:

```text
Confident VR adoption, backed by expert IT support.
```

The company presents its core work as removing XR operational friction through:

```text
procurement
provisioning
MDM setup
custom kitting
RMA and repair
fleet management
remote/on-site technical support
vendor services
```

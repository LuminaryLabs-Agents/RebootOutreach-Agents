# ICP and Personas

Date: 2026-06-24

## Highest-fit ICPs

### 1. XR software vendors and training platforms

Fit reason:

```text
Reboot Imagine offers vendor services that reduce provisioning, deployment, technical support, and white-label support burdens.
```

Buyer personas:

```text
CEO
Founder
VP Customer Success
Head of Implementations
VP Product
Director of Customer Operations
Implementation Manager
Partnerships Lead
```

Priority examples in this run:

```text
Strivr
Osso VR
Virti
Motive.io
Prisms
Acadicus
```

Fit reason:

```text
Medical simulation contexts require reliable device readiness, support, compliance awareness, and operational control.
```

Buyer personas:

```text
Director of Simulation
Clinical Simulation Director
Digital Health Program Manager
Director of Medical Education
```

Fit reason:


```text
Director of Immersive Learning
Director of Academic Technology
Director of Simulation
XR Lab Director
AV/IT Manager
Endpoint Manager
```

Priority examples in this run:

```text
King's College London

```

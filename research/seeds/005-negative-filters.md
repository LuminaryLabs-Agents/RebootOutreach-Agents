# Negative Filters

Date: 2026-06-24

## Reject or deprioritize

```text
consumer VR gaming only
headset product reviews
SteamVR gameplay
Oculus Store listings
Beat Saber content
student-only project
single demo with no deployment signal
NFT or crypto metaverse hype
AR face filters
adult entertainment
unverified lead directories
AI-generated listicles
```

## Hold rather than reject

```text
historical VR lab source
outside US/Canada but strategically relevant
XR vendor with unclear current deployment/support burden
medical/education context needing compliance review
contact name visible but current role unverified
```

## Search negative terms to test carefully

```text
-gameplay
-review
-Steam
-Beat Saber
-NFT
-token
-consumer app
```

Do not use negative terms so aggressively that legitimate location-based entertainment, simulation, or training operators are filtered out.

# Prompt 000: Reboot Imagine Research Goals

## Title

```text
Reboot Imagine Outreach Research Goals
```

## Mission

Define the research mission for building a validated cold-email lead-generation system for Reboot Imagine.

This prompt establishes the strategic foundation.

Do not gather leads yet.

Do not enrich contacts yet.

Do not prepare outreach yet.

Do not send emails.

The goal is to make sure every future agent run understands:

```text
who Reboot Imagine serves
what pain Reboot Imagine solves
which markets are highest fit
which leads should be prioritized
which validation gates must exist before outreach
```

```text
Reboot Imagine
https://www.rebootimagine.com/
```


## Required validation gates

Future lead work must use these gates.

### Gate 1: Source validity

A lead source must be public, relevant, and captured with a URL.

Required fields:

```text
source_url
source_type
organization_name
collected_at
notes
```

Fail if:

```text
source is private
source is guessed
source cannot be reviewed later
source is unrelated to XR, training, simulation, education, healthcare, enterprise operations, or vendor support
```

### Gate 2: Organization fit

The organization must match at least one ICP.

Required fields:

```text
ICP cluster
reason for fit
visible XR signal
visible operational need
recent activity signal
```

Fail if:

```text
no clear XR relevance
consumer-only VR/gaming context
student-only project
inactive public presence
no buyer or program owner can be inferred
```

### Gate 3: Contact fit

The contact must plausibly own the problem.

Required fields:

```text
contact_name
contact_title
organization
role_relevance
source_url
```

Fail if:

```text
contact is too junior
contact is unrelated to XR, training, simulation, IT, innovation, operations, customer success, or implementation
contact association cannot be verified
```

### Gate 4: Email confidence

No lead should be outreach-ready without email validation.

Required fields:

```text
email
email_source
email_validation_status
email_validation_method
domain_match_status
```

Fail if:

```text
email is guessed without review
email syntax is invalid
email domain does not match the organization
email is disposable
email is high-risk without manual review
```

### Gate 5: Compliance review

Every outreach-ready lead must have a compliance check.

Required fields:

```text
country_or_region
business_relevance
opt_out_plan
suppression_checked
sender_identity_ready
```

Fail if:

```text
business relevance is unclear
suppression list was not checked
opt-out path is missing
sender identity is unclear
jurisdiction risk is unresolved
```

### Gate 6: Outreach readiness

A lead is send-ready only when it has:

```text
ICP fit
validated organization
validated contact
validated email
clear trigger or pain signal
segment
priority score
personalization note
recommended messaging angle
human review status
```

No automated sending is authorized by this prompt.

## Scoring model

Use a 100-point model.

```text
ICP fit: 30
trigger strength: 20
pain likelihood: 15
buyer-role fit: 10
data quality: 10
ecosystem alignment: 5
timing/accessibility: 5
engagement history: 5
```

Priority bands:

```text
80-100 = Priority A
65-79 = Priority B
50-64 = Priority C
0-49 = hold or reject
```
